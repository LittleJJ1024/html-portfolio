The Best Movies According to Vedang

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sholay

(A Hindi movie) This is a classic movie from the '70s which I love, partly due to the amazing acting in it. I could watch it several more times and not get bored of it.

The Sound of Music

One of the best English movies of all time in my opinion. It has a great story, and, being a musical, features many amazing songs.

Anand

(Another Hindi movie) I like this movie because it teaches an important life lesson: to always stay joyful no matter the circumstances
